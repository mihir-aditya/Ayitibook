## 💳 CREDIT SCORE SYSTEM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Scale: 300 - 850

Factors:
  • Payment History (40%)     → +400 max
  • Account Age (20%)         → +200 max
  • Credit Utilization (20%)  → +150 max
  • Milestone Completion (20%)→ +20 each

Eligibility: Score ≥ 400

Credit Tiers:
  0-67    → Not Eligible ($0 limit)
  68-79   → Starter tier ($500 limit)
  80-89   → Standard tier ($2,000 limit)
  90+     → Premium tier ($10,000 limit)
